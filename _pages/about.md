## Current Role

I am a **Senior Applied Science Manager** at **Amazon Search**, where I lead initiatives in information retrieval and product search. My work focuses on leveraging machine learning and AI to enhance customer search experiences and drive innovation in e-commerce technology.

## Academic Contributions

As a **Lecturer at UC Berkeley's School of Information**, I teach:
- **Fundamentals of Machine Learning**
- **Introduction to Statistical Theory**

I'm passionate about educating the next generation of data scientists and engineers, emphasizing both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

## Research & Expertise

Prior to my current role at Amazon Search, I spent several years in **Alexa**, working on:
- Natural Language Understanding (NLU)
- Conversational AI systems
- Voice interaction design

I hold advanced degrees from UC Berkeley:
- **Ph.D.** in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science
- **M.S.** in Statistics
- **M.S.** in Financial Engineering

## Ongoing Research

I actively collaborate with Ph.D. students at the **University of Washington** on cutting-edge research in **Generative AI**, exploring novel applications and implications of large language models.
